Basil Smash

Elegance in Every Sip

The Basil Smash is the epitome of sophistication, where simplicity meets artistry. Vibrant green hues cascade through its crystal-clear form, a reflection of nature’s grace and bounty. This cocktail is a symphony of herbal notes, kissed by the zest of citrus and carried by the smooth embrace of gin.

East 8 Hold Up

Tropical Elegance Elevated

The East 8 Hold Up is a dazzling blend of tropical and citrus flavors, embodying elegance with a playful edge. This modern cocktail harmonizes the tangy sweetness of pineapple and passion fruit with the tartness of lime and the botanical depth of gin. Its vibrant, sunset-inspired hue hints at the bold and refreshing experience within.

Boulevardier

Timeless Sophistication

The Boulevardier is a timeless blend of bourbon, vermouth, and Campari, offering rich complexity and classic sophistication in every sip. Its deep amber hue and bold, harmonious flavors evoke effortless elegance, blending tradition with modern luxury.

Savoia Americano

Effortless elegance in every bubble

The Savoia Americano combines vermouth's herbal richness with soda water's crisp effervescence, creating a ruby-red cocktail of timeless charm and elegant refreshment—a true toast to old-world sophistication.
